Google Refine

About Google Refine

Google Refine is a power tool that allows you to load data, understand it, clean it up, reconcile it internally, and augment it with data coming from Freebase and other web sources. All with the comfort and privacy of your own computer.

It was originally developed by Metaweb Technologies, Inc.. Metaweb was acquired by Google, Inc., in July 2010.
